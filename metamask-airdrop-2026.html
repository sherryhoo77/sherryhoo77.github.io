What Is the MetaMask MASK Airdrop?

MetaMask, the world's most popular Ethereum wallet with over 30 million active users, is launching its native MASK token in 2026. The token will be distributed to users based on their MetaMask Rewards points — a points system that has been quietly tracking user activity across swaps, bridges, and ecosystem interactions.

If you already use MetaMask, you are likely already earning points. The key is to understand which actions earn the most points and maximize your activity before the snapshot.

Key fact: MetaMask Rewards points are earned retroactively — meaning activity you've already done may already be counted. Enable the Rewards dashboard in MetaMask Settings to see your current balance.

How to Check Your Current Points

1

Open MetaMask extension → click the three dots menu (⋮)

2

Go to Settings → Experimental

3

Enable MetaMask Rewards toggle

4

Return to the main screen — you'll see a Rewards tab showing your current point balance

How to Maximize Your MASK Airdrop

1. Use MetaMask Swaps Regularly

MetaMask Swaps is the in-wallet DEX aggregator. Swapping tokens through MetaMask (not through external sites) is the primary way to earn Rewards points.

  • Swap at least 3–5 times per week
  • Even small amounts ($5–10) count — consistency matters more than size
  • Swap across different token pairs (ETH↔USDC, ETH↔DAI, etc.) to show variety

2. Prioritize Linea Network (Highest Multiplier)

Linea is MetaMask's own Layer 2 blockchain. Activity on Linea receives the highest point multiplier of any chain. This is the single most important optimization.

Action: Bridge $20–50 from Ethereum to Linea via the MetaMask Bridge feature. Then do 3–4 swaps per week on Linea specifically.

3. Use MetaMask Bridge

Bridging assets between chains through MetaMask Bridge earns additional points. Bridge at least once per week:

  • Ethereum → Linea (highest value)
  • Ethereum → Base
  • Arbitrum → Linea

4. Connect to dApps Across Categories

Connect your MetaMask wallet to at least one dApp in each category:

  • DEX: Uniswap, Aerodrome, Velodrome
  • Lending: Aave, Compound, Moonwell
  • NFT: OpenSea, Blur
  • DeFi: Curve, Convex

Anti-Sybil Rules for MetaMask

Never do this: Create multiple MetaMask wallets doing identical activity. Consensys (MetaMask's developer) uses on-chain analysis to detect and exclude multi-wallet farming. One wallet, consistent use, real transactions.
